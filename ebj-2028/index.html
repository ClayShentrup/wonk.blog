Methodology

Election by Jury simulation per electionbyjury.org. 100 citizens randomly selected from a demographically stratified national sample (region, race, education, age, gender proportional to U.S. Census). 14-day structured deliberation with candidate presentations, expert briefings, cross-examination, small-group deliberation, and final secret ballot. Score voting 0–5. Ideology modeled on 2D economic/social axis. Deliberation dynamics: information gain (declining name-recognition bias), peer influence (weighted by ideological proximity and persuasiveness), and scrutiny effects. Candidate attribute estimates per March 2026 analysis.
